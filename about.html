About us

Welcome, we are very happy to have you!

VENDAR is the first site of its kind to serve medical sources to you on a golden plate.

Inspired by the idea of having all medical sources available in a single platform, we, a group of enthusiastic medical students, created this site.

You will find lots of the most reliable, high quality video explanations for all the topics covered during the academic years of medical school. And even more is yet to come; stay tuned!

Note: The videos are not restricted to a certain university curriculum so make sure you are studying from the source that best suits you.

Each subject is arranged into subtopics, in which you can find playlists explained by a bunch of the best professors on YouTube.

For more illustrations and overviews, you will also find a separate section for extra sources and revisions available per subject.

If you can't find your favorite doctor or know a better source for a certain topic, feel free to tell us. Your suggestions are always valuable!
